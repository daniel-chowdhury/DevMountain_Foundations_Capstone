Shoot the ball toward the goal!!

Welcome to my amateur soccer shooting game. First choose the angle of the ball to shoot toward, anything between 75 degree and -75 degree. Then shoot the ball toward the goal on the farthest right, avoiding the goalkeeper. If you score, the goalkeeper will move faster, the goal will move to a random location vertically, and score will increment by 1. If the ball bounces off the border and hits the goal, score will increment by 4.
