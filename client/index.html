Shoot the ball toward the goal!!

Welcome to my amateur soccer shooting game, part of my DevMountain Foundation Capstone project. Rules are simple. First choose the angle of the ball to shoot toward, anything between 75 degree and 5 degree, and -5 degree and -75 degree. Then just shoot! If you score, the goalkeeper thing will speed up. The ball will bounce off the boundary. The goalpost is on the right.
